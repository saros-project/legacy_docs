About Saros

You want to know more about how Saros can better your daily work?

You may want to have a look at

  • the features supported by Saros,
  • some screenshots showing you around Saros' user interface,
  • an easy-to-understand explanation of Saros' special host role, or
  • a collection of what others say about Saros.

If want to know more about Saros (e.g. concerning setting up a process that makes use of Saros), please do not hesitate and drop us a line.

You are interested in the Saros project itself?

Saros is an Open Source project that started at Freie Universität Berlin back in 2006, based on an idea of Stephan Salinger (Product Owner). In case you want to help us making Saros even better, feel free to inform yourself and get in contact with us.
