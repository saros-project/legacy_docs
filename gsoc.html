Google Summer of Code

Former years

Saros was one of 137 mentoring organizations of the Google Summer of Code 2015 (see our archived page).

Current year and future

Unfortunately, for a lack of mentoring capacity we won't apply in 2016. Please see the official GSoC page for further information and -- starting from 2016-02-29 -- all the other cool open-source projects you can participate in.

We hope this situation will change in the future, and we're looking forward to GSoC 2017.

Contribute

If you want to contribute to Saros anyway, feel free to dive in our introduction and get in contact with us.
